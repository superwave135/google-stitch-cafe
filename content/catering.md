## Intro
We cater meetings, workshops, and office mornings within a 5km radius. Everything is made in our kitchen the same morning it's delivered. Order by 2:00pm the day before; for the Full Spread, give us two working days.

## Packages

For meetings that just need good coffee.
- A 2.5L flask of batch-brew filter per 8 people
- Oat and dairy milk, cups, and sugar included
- Add a flask of chai for $12

### The Morning Tea — $16 per person
The one most offices reorder.
- Batch-brew filter as above
- Two bakery items per person (croissants, scones, cookie of the day)
- Seasonal fruit platter
- Minimum 6 people

### The Full Spread — $28 per person
For workshops, planning days, and celebrations.
- Everything in The Morning Tea
- Sandwich platter (halloumi and roast vegetable, chicken schnitzel)
- Fresh orange juice and house lemonade
- Minimum 10 people, two working days' notice
