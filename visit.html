How to find us

We're the corner shopfront with the deep-green window frames, halfway down Chalk Lane, with the fig tree visible through the side gate. If you can see the takeaway menu lettered on the glass, you're in the right place.

Good to know

The courtyard is dog-friendly; inside is not (sorry — kitchen rules). Big tables can be reserved for groups of six or more by email.

Getting here

  • On foot or bike: bike racks are directly outside; the courtyard has space for prams.
  • By train: eight minutes' walk from Weaver Street station — exit toward the market, then second left.
  • By bus: routes 14 and 62 stop at the top of Chalk Lane.
  • By car: street parking on Weaver Street is free for the first hour; there's a paid car park behind the market, four minutes' walk.
